Before you book

Your questions,
answered

  • A regular manicure pushes back or clips the cuticle. Russian manicure uses precision electric drill bits to remove only the dead tissue — leaving living skin completely untouched. The result is a visibly cleaner, tighter nail bed and gel that adheres with zero lifting. That's why it lasts up to 4 weeks without chipping.
  • Premature lifting almost always comes down to incomplete cuticle removal or improper nail prep. The Russian technique eliminates both. The difference is usually visible by day 3 — and unmistakable by week 3.
  • Yes — when performed correctly, Russian manicure is safer than traditional methods. It removes only dead tissue using precision drill bits, leaving living skin and nail matrix untouched. The result is healthier nails over time.
  • Every nail instrument is autoclave-sterilized and sealed in individual sterile pouches. We open your set in front of you at the start of each appointment. Files and buffers are single-use only — disposed of after each client.
  • Russian Gel Manicure: approximately 2 hours. Smart Gel Pedicure: approximately 2 hours. Both together: 3.5–4 hours (we recommend booking on separate days). Your appointment time is your start time — private studio, no waiting.
